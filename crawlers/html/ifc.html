Q&A with Broken Social Scene’s Kevin Drew & dir. Steven Chung, moderated by Pitchfork's Ryan Schreiber on Fri, May 30 at 7:15 p.m.

BEFORE SUNRISE

30th Anniversary engagement of Richard Linklater's luminous tale of love and time, starring Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy, now playing, with sequels BEFORE SUNSET and BEFORE MIDNIGHT following on June 6 and 13.

RAN

A 40th anniversary 4K restoration of Kurosawa’s Oscar-winning adaptation of Shakespeare’s King Lear, now playing!

TO DIE FOR

Post-screening book signing and Q&A w/author Joyce Maynard and Rachel Syme (The New Yorker) Mon, June 2 at 7:00

Shudder Showcase: BEST WISHES TO ALL

Voted "Scariest Feature Film" at this year's Overlook Film Festival, this off-the-wall Japanese shocker has a sneak preview June 3 at 7pm! Tickets on sale now.

GARLAND JEFFREYS: THE KING OF IN BETWEEN

Opens Fri, June 6. Q&As with dir. Claire Jeffreys and special guests Fri, June 6-Sat, June 7 at 7:30 p.m.

IT'S PAT: THE MOVIE

Q&A with star Julia Sweeney Thu, June 12 at 7:00 p.m. Part of our queer series programmed by Elizabeth Purchell and KJ Shepherd, Cruising the Movies.

GOODFELLAS

35th Anniversary 4K Restoration of Scorsese's classic gangster film now playing.
